# Business Goals

## Purpose

This document defines the business objectives that the Photography Business Platform aims to achieve.

These goals provide direction for business analysis, software design, implementation priorities, and future decision-making throughout the project lifecycle.

---

# Business Goals

## BG-01 — Establish a Professional Online Presence

Provide a modern web platform that enables the photographer to present a professional digital identity through portfolio presentation, business information, and photography services.

---

## BG-02 — Convert Visitors into Clients

Enable visitors to discover photography services, evaluate available packages, submit inquiries, and request bookings through a structured and user-friendly workflow.

---

## BG-03 — Streamline Booking Management

Replace informal booking communication with a structured booking process that supports request submission, review, scheduling, booking confirmation, booking updates, and booking completion.

---

## BG-04 — Centralize Business Information

Maintain photography services, packages, client information, bookings, payments, invoices, and inquiries within a single business management platform.

---

## BG-05 — Support Flexible Photography Services

Support both predefined photography packages and custom photography service requests, allowing the photographer to define or modify service details, pricing, and session arrangements before or after booking confirmation when necessary.

---

## BG-06 — Manage Payments Outside the Platform

Support manual recording and management of payments completed outside the platform while maintaining an accurate financial record of deposits, subsequent payments, remaining balances, and payment history.

---

## BG-07 — Automate Invoice Generation

Generate professional invoices after each confirmed payment, maintain a complete invoice history, and deliver invoices to clients by email.

---

## BG-08 — Improve Administrative Efficiency

Reduce administrative effort by organizing bookings, inquiries, payments, invoices, and client information within a single administrative dashboard.

---

## BG-09 — Support Business Growth

Provide a software architecture that is maintainable, extensible, and capable of supporting future enhancements without requiring fundamental architectural changes.

---

## BG-10 — Demonstrate Professional Software Engineering

Develop a production-oriented application that demonstrates Domain-Driven Design (DDD), Test-Driven Development (TDD), Clean Architecture, comprehensive documentation, and modern software engineering best practices.
