# Project Vision

## Purpose

This document defines the long-term vision of the Photography Business Platform.

It describes the business problem the platform addresses, the value it delivers, the desired future state, and the overall scope of the project. The vision serves as a foundation for requirements analysis, architectural decisions, and future development.

---

## Problem Statement

Professional photographers often rely on social media platforms to present their work and communicate with potential clients. While these platforms are effective for marketing, they are not designed to manage a photography business.

Client inquiries, appointment scheduling, service discussions, and booking confirmations are typically handled through phone calls or messaging applications, resulting in fragmented communication, inefficient scheduling, and increased administrative effort.

Additionally, portfolios, service information, pricing details, and client interactions are often scattered across multiple channels, making it difficult to provide a consistent and professional client experience.

---

## Vision Statement

The Photography Business Platform aims to provide a single, professional web platform that enables photographers to establish a strong digital business presence while managing their photography services efficiently.

The platform integrates portfolio presentation, service management, client inquiry management, and appointment booking into a unified solution that supports the complete client journey—from discovering the photographer's work to requesting, managing, and completing photography bookings.

---

## Business Value

For photographers, the platform provides a centralized business management solution that improves professionalism, reduces administrative effort, streamlines business operations, and increases opportunities to convert visitors into clients.

For clients, the platform offers a convenient and transparent booking experience that simplifies discovering photography services, submitting booking requests, communicating service requirements, and tracking the booking process from inquiry to completion.

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it:

- Provides a professional online presence for photographers.
- Enables clients to request photography services through a structured booking process.
- Reduces manual appointment management and communication overhead.
- Supports real-world photography business operations in a production environment.
- Delivers a secure, maintainable, and production-ready web application.
- Demonstrates professional software engineering practices through comprehensive documentation, automated testing, Domain-Driven Design, Clean Architecture, and Test-Driven Development.

---

## Project Scope

The platform focuses on supporting the business activities of a professional photographer, including:

- Portfolio presentation.
- Photography service management.
- Client inquiry management.
- Appointment booking and booking management.
- Client information management.
- Payment tracking.
- Invoice generation.
- Business administration.

The first version of the platform is designed for a single photography business operated by one photographer.

The project does not aim to become:

- A social media platform.
- An image or video editing application.
- An online marketplace for photographers.
- A real-time messaging platform.
- An online payment gateway.
