# Project Roadmap

## Purpose

This document defines the high-level development roadmap for the Photography Business Platform.

It provides a strategic view of the project's implementation by dividing the work into incremental Scrum sprints. Each sprint delivers a complete and measurable outcome that contributes to building a production-ready application.

Detailed implementation tasks are maintained in the Sprint Backlog.

---

## Development Approach

The project follows an incremental development approach based on Scrum.

Development progresses through consecutive sprints, beginning with business understanding and software design before implementation. This approach reduces technical risk, improves architectural consistency, and ensures that implementation is driven by well-defined business requirements.

---

**Major Deliverables**

- Documentation structure established.

- Business assumptions documented.
- Core architectural decisions approved.
- Initial project roadmap.

## Expected Outcome

Upon completion of this roadmap, the project will deliver a production-ready Photography Business Platform for a single photography business.

The platform will provide portfolio presentation, photography service management, appointment booking, client inquiry management, payment tracking, invoice generation, and business administration while following Domain-Driven Design, Test-Driven Development, Clean Architecture, and modern software engineering practices.
